Petrol scooters mean fuel queues and licence checks. Electric is simpler:

- **No fuel** — bikes arrive fully charged and top up at any outlet.
- **No licence** — electric scooters are licence-free for tourists.
- **Quiet and clean** — zero emissions through the old town and along the beach.

With up to 262 km of range you can explore all day and plug in overnight. Cheaper than taxis, and a lot more fun.
