Đà Nẵng was made for two wheels. Start early along **My Khe beach**, ride north to the **Son Tra peninsula**, then loop back past the **Marble Mountains**.

The full loop is about 40 km — easy on any of our scooters and well within a single charge. Stop for coffee at the foot of Son Tra and watch the fishing boats come in.

Helmet on, key twisted, and you are off. No licence needed.
